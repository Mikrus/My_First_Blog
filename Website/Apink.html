Anime Matsuri's Best Year

February 22nd, 2022 by April Joseph

Anime Matsuri is an annual anime convention that first began in 2007. John and Deniece Leigh started the convention in the Hilton Americas hotel and over the years it gained popularity,
allowing it to be held continuously at the George R. Brown Center in downtown Houston, TX. They have had several foreign guests attend their anime convention and in recent years,
the overall attendance has been well over 25,000 people! Some of the notable guests that have attended the convention are:

Vic Mignogna (American Voice Actor), Johnny Yong Bosch (American Actor & Voice Actor), Morning Musume (Japanese girl group), Cristina Vee (American Voice Actress), and Yua Mikami (Japanese AV Actress & idol).

What Year Was The Best For Anime Matsuri?

Out of all years, Anime Matsuri has hosted its anime convention, I would say that 2019 was their most notable and best year!
There were tons of things in 2019 to look forward to that you can find at the convention. Some people like to go for the cosplay meet-ups, others like to go for the car reactor shows. There are always panels, usually a maid cafe, and you could always look forward to Voice Actors. What separated 2019's convention from the rest was the big names in one year along with the special services.

Who Were Those Special Guests?

My favorite guest was Apink, a Korean idol girl group. I had heard one or two songs before attending the convention concert,
but it wasn't until I saw the group in person that I was taken aback by how amazing they were. Apink has won many awards, including the Best Celebrity award for 2017 and the Best Female Group award in 2018. It was the first time a Korean group as
popular as Apink had attended the convention. The next notable guest was Ladybaby, a Japanese Kawaii Metal Music group.
In their genre of music, they are well known and their former member, Ladybeard had a hand in their success.
